Shopping Bag

Our Store

We at Carey's Comics and Collectibles know that it is a time period where the internet has become one of the primary ways people shop. We also realize that it is sometimes difficult to shop for certain products, like a comic book online, because people are used to an in personal one on one experience where buyers can interact with each other and learn of new and exciting products through each other.

It is because of this need that we have worked together to create a site that not only has the best buying experience, but the best shopping experience as well. We have combined our goals of creating Excellence in Customer Service with Ease of Ordering, in hopes that we can deliver you what you need in the most cost effective and efficient way possible.

Due to the growing pandemic due to the Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) we are doing our best to serve you. We understand that many of you are stuck inside your homes and we are working extra hard to deliver you some small comfort. In order to do so, we are doing the following in order to better serve you, as well as our employees during this trying time.

Created Extended Hours

▪ We know that being home makes it difficult so we are offering extended hours of customer service so that you may receive help with your online orders.

▪ We have established a 24 hour live chat service.

▪ Hired more people in order to help ship your product faster.

Special Sales

▪ To make things easier we will now have more special sales events. See our weekly deals for more information!
